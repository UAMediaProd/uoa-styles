Buttons can be buttons or p or div tags with the .adx-button class. To add colour, add the colour to the .adx-button class - e.g. adx-button-brand-red.

Persona Cue

Base Persona

Sarah Lastnamington

At work, Sarah notices quite often that a few teachers leave their dirty mugs in the sink without washing them up. While this may seem like a trivial action to be annoyed about, it affects Sarah’s thoughts about their professional identities. When she sees them walking away from their dirty mugs, she associates them with being lazy, irresponsible, and inconsiderate towards others. Even if they are excellent teachers, their personal brand has been tarnished by their behaviour in the staffroom.
